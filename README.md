# ASOS Product & Sales Analysis

An exploratory data analysis project examining ASOS product data to uncover trends in pricing, categories, discounting behaviour, and consumer demand signals within the fast-fashion e-commerce space.

## Overview

ASOS is one of the UK's largest online fashion retailers, offering thousands of products across clothing, footwear, and accessories. This project analyses ASOS product listing data to identify patterns that would be relevant to commercial, buying, or merchandising teams — demonstrating the kind of data-driven thinking applicable to retail analyst roles.

## Key Questions Explored

- How are products distributed across categories and price points?
- What discounting patterns exist, and which categories are most heavily marked down?
- Are there correlations between pricing, brand, and product ratings?
- What does the product mix reveal about ASOS's target customer segments?

## Skills Demonstrated

- Data cleaning and preprocessing with `pandas`
- Exploratory data analysis (EDA) on real e-commerce product data
- Visualisation of retail trends with `matplotlib` and `seaborn`
- Descriptive statistics and group-based aggregation
- Translating analytical findings into business-relevant insights
